Lagazzuolo

Lombardy, Italy

Author: Alessandro Ontani

Lagazzuolo is located at the Alpeggio homonymous on the southern side of the Chiareggio valley. The panorama shows from the north to the east the splendid chain that includes, starting from the left: Pizzo Fora (3363 m), Pizzo Trevoggia (3441 m) looking towards which the Longoni refuge can be distinguished (m. 2450), Malenco lace (m. 3438) and Sasso d'Entova (3329 m). At the point where the former Rifugio Entova (2957 m) is seen on the ridge, the top of the Roseg comes out (3920 m). We then continue with the Sasso Nero (2919 m) and the Sasso Moro (m. 3108), up to the Scalino lace (3323 m) which closes the sequence. On the opposite side they stand starting from the left: the M. ARMA (2909 m), the top of the Duke (2968 m), La Punta Rosalba (m. 2809) to whose right through the Bocchel of the dog (m. 2458) You can go down to Lake Pirola (2263 m) and subsequently to the Porro-Gerli (1960 m) and twenty (m. 1965) shelters.
