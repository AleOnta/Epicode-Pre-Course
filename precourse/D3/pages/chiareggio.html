Chiareggio

Lombardy, Italy

Author: Alessandro Ontani

The village of Chiareggio belongs to the municipality of Chiesa in Valmalenco, in the province of Sondrio, region Lombardia. The village of Chiareggio is 7,51 kilometers far from the same town of Chiesa in Valmalenco to whom it belongs. To the municipality of Chiesa in Valmalenco also belong the localities of Alpe Lago (3,53 km), Barchi (3,85 km), Braccia (4,89 km), Carotte (5,97 km), Case sparse (-- km), Curlo (1,28 km), Località Palo Lungo (5,90 km), Località Prati Pedrana (4,01 km), Pian del Lupo (9,25 km), Primolo (1,04 km), San Giuseppe (3,79 km), Valrosera (2,58 km), Vassalini (1,39 km).
